Our Story

Seattle's Neighborhood
Auto Shop

G & M Auto Repair was built on a simple idea — treat every customer like family and every car like it's your own. Located on Rainier Ave S in Seattle, we've grown from a small operation into one of the neighborhood's most trusted shops.

We specialize in complex repairs that other shops turn away — engine swaps, transmission rebuilds, and everything in between. If you've been told your car is too far gone, bring it to us.

Our team communicates every step of the way. You'll always know what we found, what it costs, and why it matters — before we do anything.
