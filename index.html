Our Story

A Seattle Family,
Serving Seattle Families

G & M Family Auto Repair was founded in 1998 by George and Maria Vasquez with a simple belief: people deserve a mechanic they can actually trust. What started as a two-bay shop in South Seattle has grown into a full-service center — but the family values haven't changed.

Today, their son Marco and a team of ASE-certified technicians carry on the tradition of honest, quality work. Many clients have been with us 15+ years — now bringing in their kids' first cars.
